What it does

Lets investors elect flat separate taxation of 14–30% by bracket on cash dividends from qualifying high-payout listed companies, instead of aggregation at up to 45%. fact Applies to dividends received from Jan 1, 2026 through 2028; opt-in at filing; ETFs, funds and REITs excluded. fact Read as a Korea-discount measure: it raises the controlling shareholder's incentive to pay out. est.
